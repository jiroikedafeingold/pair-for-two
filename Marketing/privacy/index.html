Pair for Two

Two-phone cribbage

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Pair for Two does not collect, store, or share any personal data. There are no accounts to create for local play, no analytics, no advertising, and no third‑party tracking of any kind.

What stays on your device

  • Your chosen display name, colour, and preferences are saved only on your device.
  • Game progress (so you can resume an interrupted match) is stored only on your device.

Playing nearby

“Play nearby” connects two phones directly using Apple’s MultipeerConnectivity (Bluetooth and peer‑to‑peer Wi‑Fi). Game messages travel encrypted, device to device. There is no server, and nothing is sent over the internet or stored by us.

Playing online

“Play online” uses Apple’s Game Center to match you with a friend. That connection is handled by Apple under Apple’s Privacy Policy. Pair for Two itself does not collect or retain any of that information.

Children’s privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
